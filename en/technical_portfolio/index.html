 MESWIFI, S.L., Palau-solita i Plegamans (Spain)

URL:www.meswifi.com

MESWIFI was my own company with my partner from 2012 to 2016. We created a Wireless Internet Service Provider (WISP) based on Ubiquiti, Mikrotik, FreeBSD, Cisco, and Ceragon, comprised of over 3,500 nodes. The network consisted of a redundant backbone of point-to-point radio links that, at peak times, reached a bandwidth consumption of 1 Gbps. MESWIFI's network coverage extended across the provinces of Barcelona and Tarragona. Our BGP exchange point was the Jazztel data center in the Zona Franca of Barcelona. There, we had two racks with Mikrotik RouterOS routers, Layer 3 and Layer 2 Cisco Catalyst switches, and servers for IDS, DNS, email, and VPBX operations. The servers ran FreeBSD, OpenBSD, and Debian Linux.
