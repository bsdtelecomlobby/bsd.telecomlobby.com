Backhaul points of presence

We need to service the five towers with redundant backhaul technology. We are using two unlicensed bands: 60 GHz for the primary point-to-point connection and 5 GHz for redundant failover backups. Geographic points are estimated, except for the "MAIN TOWER," which appears to already exist. Satellite topographic data is used for terrain southveying and radio coverage.

Complete backhaul network design

Four point-to-point backhaul links, operating at 60 GHz with automatic 5 GHz backup, prevent damage from torrential rain and connect the MAIN TOWER to towers A, B, C, and D. Another four point-to-point backhaul links, operating at 5 GHz, prevent hardware failures by connecting A to B, B to C, C to D, and D to A.
